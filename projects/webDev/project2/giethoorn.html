Giethoorn

Giethoorn is a small, peaceful village located in the northeastern part of the Netherlands. Famous for its boat-filled canals, Giethoorn is often referred to as the "Venice of the North." The village offers a tranquil experience, with no roads, only waterways.

Activities to Experience in Giethoorn

Boat Tours Through the Canals

One of the most popular activities in Giethoorn is taking a boat tour through its picturesque canals. You can rent a traditional "whisper boat" (a silent, electric boat) or join a guided boat tour to explore the charming waterways, bridges, and beautiful thatched-roof cottages that define the village’s landscape.

Visit the Museum Giethoorn ‘t Olde Maat Uus

Learn about the history and culture of Giethoorn by visiting this local museum, which showcases the traditional way of life in the village. Explore exhibits on farming, peat-cutting, and the water-centric lifestyle that has shaped Giethoorn’s history over the centuries.

Dining in Canal-Side Restaurants

After a day of exploring, unwind in one of Giethoorn's cozy canal-side restaurants and cafés. Savor local Dutch delicacies or enjoy international cuisine while soaking in the peaceful atmosphere and idyllic views of the village.

Take a Horse-Drawn Carriage Ride

Experience the village in a unique way by taking a horse-drawn carriage ride through the picturesque landscape. This leisurely ride offers a nostalgic perspective on Giethoorn and its surroundings.

Bike the Scenic Routes

Rent a bike and explore the scenic paths surrounding Giethoorn. The flat terrain makes it easy to navigate, and you can enjoy views of the countryside, fields, and nearby lakes.

Attend Open-Air Concerts

During the summer months, Giethoorn often hosts open-air concerts featuring local musicians. Enjoy live music while relaxing by the canals and soaking in the village’s charming atmosphere.
