Zaanse Schans

Zaanse Schans offers a charming blend of history, culture, and traditional Dutch craftsmanship, making it a must-visit destination for anyone interested in experiencing the Netherlands' rural heritage.

  • Catharina Hoeve Cheese Farm

Activities to Experience in Zaanse Schans

Explore the Historic Windmills

Zaanse Schans is famous for its well-preserved, working windmills. Visit windmills like De Kat, a paint mill, and Het Jonge Schaap, a sawmill, to see how these iconic Dutch structures operate. You can even climb to the top for a panoramic view of the surrounding countryside.

Visit the Wooden Shoe Workshop

Learn about the traditional Dutch craft of wooden clog-making at the Klompenmakerij. Watch live demonstrations of how wooden shoes, or clogs, are carved, and try on a pair yourself. The workshop also has a variety of clogs on display, ranging from functional to artistic.

Tour the Cheese Farm

Stop by the Catharina Hoeve Cheese Farm to discover the art of Dutch cheese-making. Watch cheese being made the traditional way, sample a variety of locally produced cheeses like Gouda and Edam, and purchase some as a tasty souvenir.

Take a Boat Tour Along the Zaan River

Enjoy a relaxing boat tour along the Zaan River, where you can see the picturesque windmills and traditional homes from the water. This scenic ride provides a peaceful way to take in the beauty of the area.

Explore the Zaanse Museum

Dive deeper into the history and culture of the region at the Zaanse Museum, which houses artifacts and exhibits related to the Zaan region's industrial heritage. Learn about the area’s rich history in manufacturing, including its role in producing goods like cocoa, oil, and mustard.

Bike Around the Village

Rent a bike and ride through the beautiful countryside surrounding Zaanse Schans. The flat landscape and scenic paths make it an ideal spot for a leisurely bike ride, allowing you to explore more of the area’s picturesque windmills and traditional homes.
