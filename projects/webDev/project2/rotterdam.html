Rotterdam

Rotterdam is a cutting-edge city renowned for its bold architecture, vibrant cultural scene, and Europe’s largest port. From the iconic Cube Houses to its lively waterfront, the city offers a unique blend of modernity and maritime heritage.

Activities to Experience in Rotterdam

Explore the Iconic Architecture

Rotterdam is famous for its cutting-edge architecture. Visit the Cube Houses (Kubuswoningen), uniquely designed homes tilted at 45-degree angles. Explore the striking Erasmus Bridge, a symbol of the city’s modern skyline, or experience the bustling Markthal, an indoor market with a stunning arched ceiling covered in colorful artwork.

Take a Harbor Tour

Rotterdam is home to Europe’s largest port, and a harbor tour offers a fascinating glimpse into the city's maritime heritage. Explore the impressive container terminals, industrial complexes, and bustling shipping routes while learning about the vital role Rotterdam plays in global trade.

Climb the Euromast Tower

For panoramic views of the city, head to Euromast, Rotterdam’s highest observation tower. Standing at 185 meters, visitors can enjoy stunning 360-degree views from the top. For an added thrill, adventurous visitors can abseil or zipline from the tower!

Visit the Boijmans Van Beuningen Museum

Art lovers can dive into centuries of art history at the Museum Boijmans Van Beuningen, which houses a remarkable collection of European masterpieces from artists like Van Gogh, Rembrandt, and Dalí. The museum also showcases contemporary art, design, and surrealist pieces, offering something for everyone.

Walk Along Witte de Withstraat

Experience Rotterdam’s lively cultural scene by strolling down Witte de Withstraat, known for its trendy art galleries, boutiques, and street art. The street is also filled with hip cafés, bars, and restaurants, making it the perfect spot to relax and soak in the local atmosphere.

Bike Along the Maas River

Rotterdam is a bike-friendly city, and cycling along the Maas River offers beautiful views of the city’s skyline and architectural landmarks. Follow the cycling paths that lead to parks, bridges, and green spaces, or take a scenic ride through the Kralingse Bos forest and park.
