Amsterdam

Amsterdam, the capital city of the Netherlands, is famous for its beautiful canals, historical museums, and vibrant nightlife. One of the most picturesque cities in Europe, Amsterdam offers a unique blend of cultural richness and modern amenities.

  • Explore the Van Gogh Museum
  • Visit the World Class Museums
  • Take a boat tour along the canals
  • Discover the Bloemenmarkt
  • Visit the Heineken Experience
  • Visit the Royal Palace of Amsterdam

Activities to Experience in Amsterdam

Canal Cruises

Experience the beauty of Amsterdam’s canals from the water. Choose from various options, including romantic candlelight cruises, guided tours, or private boat rentals.

Visit World-Class Museums

Explore Dutch art and history at the Rijksmuseum, discover the largest collection of works by Vincent van Gogh, and walk through the hidden annex of the Anne Frank House.

Explore the Jordaan Neighborhood

Wander through this picturesque area filled with boutique shops, art galleries, and cozy cafés. Don’t miss the local markets and hidden courtyards.

Discover the Bloemenmarkt

Stroll through the famous floating flower market, where you can buy tulip bulbs and other beautiful flowers.

Visit the Heineken Experience

Discover the history of the world-famous beer brand through interactive exhibits and tastings in this historic brewery.

Visit the Royal Palace of Amsterdam

Tour this stunning 17th-century building, which serves as the city hall and a venue for royal events.
