Keukenhof Gardens

Known as the "Garden of Europe," Keukenhof is one of the largest and most beautiful flower gardens in the world. Located in Lisse, this colorful attraction is a paradise for flower enthusiasts, especially famous for its tulips.

  • Cycling Through the Tulip Fields
  • Discover the Flower Parade

Activities to Experience in Keukenhof Gardens

Stroll Through the Colorful Tulip Fields

Wander through fields filled with millions of tulips in vibrant colors and patterns. Keukenhof is home to over 7 million flowers, offering a picturesque and serene environment for guests to explore and take photos.

Enjoy a Boat Ride Along the Flower Fields

Hop on a guided boat tour that takes you through the flower-growing regions surrounding Keukenhof. Enjoy the unique perspective of the fields from the water and get an in-depth understanding of Dutch horticulture.

Cycling Through the Tulip Fields

Rent a bike from one of the many rental shops near Keukenhof and explore the surrounding tulip fields at your own pace. The flat Dutch landscape is ideal for cycling, and there are several scenic routes available for cyclists.

Discover the Flower Parade

If you visit during the flower parade season (usually in mid-April), don't miss the spectacular Bloemencorso. This event features colorful floats adorned with flowers, traveling from Haarlem to Keukenhof, offering a vibrant display of floral creativity.

Enjoy Local Culinary Delights

Savor traditional Dutch cuisine at one of the many restaurants and cafés within the park. Sample local dishes such as stroopwafels (thin waffle cookies with syrup) or herring while enjoying the scenic surroundings.
