Power Profile

BLOSSOM

The intelligent and leader of the Powerpuff Girls, Blossom is known for her strong sense of responsibility and bravery.

BUTTERCUP

The toughest and most fiery of the Powerpuff Girls, Buttercup is fearless and often takes on challenges headfirst.

BUBBLES

The cute and bubbly member of the Powerpuff Girls, Bubbles is sweet, caring, and has a unique ability to communicate with animals.

PROFFESOR UTONIUM

The kind-hearted and brilliant scientist who created the Powerpuff Girls, Professor Utonium serves as their father figure.

MOJO JOJO

The Powerpuff Girls' arch-nemesis, Mojo Jojo is an evil, super-intelligent ape with a thirst for power and world domination.

MISS BELLUM

The mayor’s assistant, Miss Bellum is intelligent, capable, and often the voice of reason, although her face is never shown on screen.

HIM

The sinister, demonic villain with a flamboyant personality who uses manipulation and dark powers to challenge the Powerpuff Girls.

MAYOR OF TOWNSVILLE

The somewhat bumbling, often clueless mayor of Townsville, he relies on the Powerpuff Girls to keep the city safe from villains and disasters.

MISS KEANE

A kind, patient, and caring kindergarten teacher who genuinely loves her students and supports the Powerpuff Girls.

FUZZY LUMPKINS

The grumpy, pink, hillbilly monster who attacks anyone who trespasses on his property.

PRINCESS MORBUCKS

The spoiled, rich little girl who wants to be a Powerpuff Girl and will do anything to make it happen.

THE GANGREEN GANG

The group of green skinned juvenile delinquents who love causing chaos around Townsville.

SEDUSA

The villainous femme fatale who uses her prehensile hair to commit crimes.

THE AMOEBA BOYS

The trio of clueless amoeba who think they're big-time criminals, but are usually harmless.

TOWNSVILLE PARK

The peaceful public park where the girls often relax or fight villains.

TOWNSVILLE CITY HALL

The Mayor’s office and center of government activities.

POKEY OAKS KINDERGARTEN

The girls' beloved school where Miss Keane teaches.

THE POWERPUFF GIRL'S HOUSE

The cozy home where Professor Utonium and the girls live together.

MOJO JOJO'S LAIR

Mojo Jojo’s evil headquarters located inside a live volcano.

TOWNSVILLE MUSEUM

The historical museum often targeted by villains for their evil schemes.

TOWNSVILLE PRISON

Where captured villains are kept after being defeated.

MALPH'S MARKET

The popular grocery store in Townsville where the girls and citizens often shop for food.

TOWNSVILLE BANK

The city’s bank that villains often attempt to rob.

CHEMICAL X LABORATORY

Where Professor Utonium experiments, and where Chemical X was created.
